Compare Invoice to City Average

Mission Background
As a Lead Financial Analyst, you are tasked with identifying regional spending anomalies. The invoice table contains every sale, and comparing each transaction against the local city average can reveal true outliers.


Analysis Requirements
Calculate the average invoice total for each city
Display each invoice alongside its city benchmark
Maintain individual invoice details while showing the aggregate

Success Criteria
Your query must return the following columns:

billing_city: The city associated with the transaction
total: The individual invoice amount
A column representing the average total for all invoices in that city
Please note: The order of results does not matter.
