user_id (string)
event_time (timestamp)
event_type (string: "page_view", "add_to_cart", "purchase")

Task (Simplified):
Instead of detecting sessions with 30-min gaps, just:
Assign fixed time windows (say, 30-minute tumbling windows).

For each (user_id, window), compute:
total number of events (event_count)
session duration (max(event_time) - min(event_time))
had_purchase (True/False if any purchase in the window)
