Longest and Shortest Track Durations

Background
The track table stores the length of every song in milliseconds. Identifying the extreme ends of our catalog helps in analyzing track variety.

Your Mission
Write a query that provides the following information:
A new column representing the maximum of milliseconds
A new column representing the minimum of milliseconds
By creating this report, you will find the duration of the longest and shortest songs, which helps the team understand the range of content available in the store.
