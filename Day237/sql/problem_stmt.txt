Second Highest Salary per Department

Mission Background
As an HR Analyst, you need to identify the second-highest earner in each department for a compensation benchmarking report. The staff_salaries_531 table holds one row per employee with their salary and department.


Analysis Requirements
Rank employees by salary within each department and return only those with a rank of exactly 2. Use dense ranking so ties at rank 1 do not skip rank 2.


Success Criteria
Your result must contain:

department
staff_name
salary
A new column representing the salary rank within the department
Filtering Rules:

Only employees ranked exactly 2 within their department
Ordering:

By department ascending
