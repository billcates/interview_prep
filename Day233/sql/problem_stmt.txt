Pivot Revenue by Product Category

Mission Background
As a Sales Analyst, you need to compare category revenue side by side for each month. The monthly_sales_365 table stores revenue in long format with one row per month and category combination.


Analysis Requirements
Transform the data into wide format so each month appears on one row with separate columns for Electronics, Clothing, and Home revenue. Also include a column for total revenue across all three categories for that month.


Success Criteria
Your result must contain:

sale_month
A new column representing total Electronics revenue for that month
A new column representing total Clothing revenue for that month
A new column representing total Home revenue for that month
A new column representing the combined total revenue for that month
Filtering Rules:

Include all months and all categories
Ordering:

By sale_month ascending
