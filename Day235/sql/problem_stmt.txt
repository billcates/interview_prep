Price Change Direction per Product Over Time

Mission Background
As a Pricing Analyst, you need to track how prices have evolved for each product over time. The price_history_659 table records price changes with effective dates.


Analysis Requirements
For each price record, retrieve the previous price for the same product and compute the absolute change. Label the direction as Increase, Decrease, or Unchanged. Exclude the very first price record for each product since there is no prior price to compare against.


Success Criteria
Your result must include the following columns:

product_name
effective_date
unit_price
A new column representing the previous price
A new column representing the price change amount
A new column classifying the direction as Increase, Decrease, or Unchanged
Filtering Rules:

Only rows with a previous price are returned (first record per product excluded)
Ordering:

By product_name ascending, then effective_date ascending
