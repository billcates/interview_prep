Top Revenue Store per Region

Mission Background
As a Retail Operations Manager, you need to identify the highest-performing store in each region. The stores_655 table holds store details and store_sales_655 records individual sales transactions.


Analysis Requirements
Compute total revenue per store, rank stores within their region by revenue, and return only the top-ranked store per region.


Success Criteria
Your result must include the following columns:

Store name
Region
A new column representing total revenue
A new column representing the rank within the region
Filtering Rules:

Only the rank 1 store per region is returned
Ordering:

By total revenue descending
